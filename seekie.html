Seekie : A Crawler Robot

So, I wanted a remote-controlled vehicle to mess around with. A car? Eh, they're everywhere. A drone? Nah, too pricey (Well... Seekie's metal chassis was kinda expensive, but not *that* much). A tank? Now we're talking! But not just any tank – a blue one! And that's how Seekie came to life. (Seekie comes from "seeker" 'cause it can move around and "seek" things!) Wait, though... how am I gonna control this thing? I don’t have a remote, and I have no idea how to make one either. But hold up... don’t I have a phone? And I do know how to make an app! Yep! that’s right! And that’s how the Seekie app was born. I can control Seekie from my phone – go forward, backward, left, right, see through its eyes [camera], and even make them blink [headlights]. Seekie can’t drive himself yet, but with his camera and ToF sensors, maibe he soon will!
